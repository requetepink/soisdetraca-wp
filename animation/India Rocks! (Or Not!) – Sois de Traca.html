India Rocks! (Or Not!)

An audiovisual piece depicting contrasting aspects of India. Commissioned by Creative Black Country for Alchemy Black Country 2017, a contemporary festival exploring the cultural links between the UK and South Asia – through live performance, talks, film, exhibitions, workshops and music.

CommissionerCreative Black Country
DateMay 2017
CategoryAnimation

 

Concept, script and voice Bobby Tiwana
Animation, design and direction Sois de Traca
Sound design Kath Shackleton
Produced by Fettle Animation

Supported by Arts Council England
