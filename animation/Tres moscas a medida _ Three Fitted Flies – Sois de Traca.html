Tres moscas a medida / Three Fitted Flies

“The film creates and recreates again its own narrative and visual space with inventiveness and constant surprises. Beguilingly simple in its visual design it engages the viewers with its quirky atmosphere, but also the very personal feelings of the main character, hidden under fly wings.” Fest Anča

Date2015
CategoryAnimation, Short Film
Tags2D, drawn, experimental, narrative

“A strong and emotional story using very simple techniques and design, giving us the feeling of how instant guilt slowly can make an obsessive compulsive disorder grow.”
Turku Animated Film Festival

“Proof that surrealism can exist within animation“ Steven Woloshen

“A mysterious accident depicted through the minimalistic visual language and absurdity of distorted dimensions.“ Be There! Corfu Animation Festival

‘Three Fitted Flies’ ranked 9th in the Top Industry Survey of 2016’s Best Animated Short Films.

 

7′ 47” / Colour / 2015.
Direction, Writing, Animation and Sound by Sois de Traca.

Summer in a small village in a plain and arid region of central Spain. Prying neighbours, mourning rituals, tediousness, extreme heat and undertones of guilt make a perfect soil for madness. The old woman will try to bring her late husband back by means of twisted rituals.

Short animated film produced during our CreArt residency in Kaunas, Lithuania. Supported by CreArt, VšĮ “Artkomas” and Culture Programme.
