Our Old Trafford

17' 43'' / Multiscreen Film Installation / 2017
Multiscreen audiovisual experience revealing the rich and diverse histories of Old Trafford neighbourhood in collaboration with Manchester Histories.

CommissionerManchester Histories , Limelight.
DateDecember 2017
CategoryInstallation, Interactive
Tagsarchival, commissioned, installation, Multiscreen, photography, public space

Created for the opening of Limelight community hub, ‘Our Old Trafford’ is a bespoke installation for their 4D Immersive Space featuring large scale projection (4 high grade projectors), LED lighting and surround sound.


Delving deep into old photo archives from Trafford Local Studies Centre, film footage from North West Film Archives and local people’s memories, the installation brings to life the industrious and friendly nature of Old Trafford through the ages.
